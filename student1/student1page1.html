Maintenance Tips

Change your oil every 5,000-7,500 miles for synthetic oil, or every 3,000 miles for conventional oil. Regular oil changes help maintain engine performance and longevity.

Check tire pressure monthly and rotate tires every 6,000-8,000 miles. Maintain proper tire pressure for optimal fuel efficiency and safety. Look for signs of uneven wear which may indicate alignment issues.

Listen for unusual brake noises and have them inspected immediately. Replace brake pads when they reach 3-4mm thickness. Check brake fluid levels every 6 months and replace it every 2 years.

Check battery terminals for corrosion every 3 months. Clean terminals with a wire brush if needed. Most car batteries last 3-5 years - consider replacement if your battery is in this age range.

Replace air filters every 15,000-30,000 miles. A clean air filter improves engine efficiency and fuel economy. Check more frequently if you drive in dusty conditions.
